ABOUT

I started learning to ride a bike with my brothers and friends when I was younger, around 14 years old, and eventually learned to travel everywhere using bike. However, since the pandemic began three years ago, we had also stopped traveling wherever we were having fun, joining a race or spending time planning to try the delicious food and breathtaking views. When biking stopped, I sidelined a selling of bike parts, clothing, and accessories to earn money and because this is also my passion. So this portfolio is something I planned as a theme for my presentation in the upcoming assessment and will eventually become my own website that will be used to increase the number of buyers of my product.
